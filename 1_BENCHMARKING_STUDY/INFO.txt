THREE EXERCISES HAVE BEEN PERFORMED WITH ANALYTICAL SOLUTIONS
1) NO REACTION: JUST SOLUTE MOVEMENT AS A CONSERVATIVE SOLUTE
2) DECAY OF SPECIES A WITH FIRST ORDER RATE OF REACTION = 3*E^-6 MOL/SEC
3) FIRST ORDER DECAY (3*E^-6 MOL/SEC) AND ZERO ORDER PRODUCTION (E^-6 MOL/SEC)

INPUT:
FILENAME IS READ FROM PHREEQCRM_INPUTFILE

OUTPUT:
FOR THE FIRST FIVE DAYS (1 TO 5 JANUARY 2024), THE MEDIA GETS SATURATED 
ON 6TH DAY SOLUTE IS ADDED VIA FERTIGATION MODULE AND CONCENTRATIONS ARE WRITTEN AT 0TH HOUR OF A DAY TO FERTIGATION_OUTPUT.TXT

VISUALIZATION:
-'1_PHREEQC_2DSOIL_BENCHMARKING_SOLUTE_MOVEMENT' GENERATES (IN THE TERMINAL) THE SOLUTE CONCENTRATIONS WRT TIME FOR CASE 1 IN CONSOLE/TERMINAL
-'2_PHREEQC_2DSOIL_BENCHMARKING_SOLUTE_BIODEGRADATION'GENERATES (IN THE TERMINAL) THE SOLUTE CONCENTRATIONS WRT TIME FOR CASE 2 and CASE 3 IN CONSOLE/TERMINAL
-FOR CASE 2 AND CASE 3, USER WILL HAVE TO CHANGE THE VALUE OF MU (FIRST ORDER DECAY RATE) AND GAMMA (ZERO ORDER PRODUCTION RATE)
